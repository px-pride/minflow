MinFlow Help

Getting Started

  • Decks are containers for your tasks. Click "Add Deck" to create one.
  • Cards are individual tasks within a deck. Select a deck and use the side panel to add cards.
  • Visual Organization - Arrange decks on the canvas by dragging them around.

Deck Operations

  • Select - Click a deck to view and manage its cards
  • Move - Click and drag to reposition decks
  • Resize - Drag the bottom-right corner handle
  • Multi-select - Click and drag on empty canvas to select multiple decks
  • Edit - Right-click a deck and choose "Edit Deck"
  • Delete - Press Delete key or use right-click menu

Card Management

  • Add Card - Select a deck, type in the side panel, and click "Add Card"
  • Complete Card - Click the "Complete" button on any card
  • Reorder - Drag and drop cards to rearrange them
  • Delete Card - Click the "Delete" button on any card

Features

  • Filters - Use the bottom toolbar to filter decks by color, shape, or size
  • Dark Mode - Toggle with the sun/moon button in the toolbar
  • Notes - Use the Notes tab in the left panel for freeform text
  • History - View your recent actions in the History tab
  • Export/Import - Save and load your workspace using the toolbar buttons

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+Z - Undo
  • Ctrl+Shift+Z / Ctrl+Y - Redo
  • Delete - Delete selected deck(s)
  • Escape - Deselect all
